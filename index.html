Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Science at the University of North Texas. My research focuses on computer vision and machine learning, specifically aiming to enhance the safety, interpretability, and robustness of computer vision systems. I completed my PhD in 2024 at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.
